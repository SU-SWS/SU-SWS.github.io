Jumpstart

The Stanford Sites Jumpstart service offers a way to create a simple, elegant website that requires little technical expertise for ongoing management at a cost well below third-party web service providers.

A Jumpstart site is a pre-packaged website solution, providing a defined package of modules, design layouts, and an easy-to-use, web-based administrative tool for content management. It is hosted on IT Services’ new Stanford Sites Drupal-hosting platform. Jumpstart sites are designed to meet common functional needs of university departments and administrative units in a visually compelling, university-branded design. A set of Stanford-branded theme options are available to choose from. Basic training, user support, and site maintenance are provided.

Ideal For:

  • Departments without trained staff or developers to implement the free Stanford Sites Drupal self-service
  • Small administrative departments or units with only a few content contributors
  • Small academic projects, laboratories, groups, and research programs with only a few content contributors

Jumpstart Academic

The Stanford Sites Jumpstart Academic service offers a way to create an academic department website, featuring complex news and announcements, events, courses, publications, and people listings. Each site requires little technical expertise for ongoing management and is available at a cost well below third-party web service providers.

A Jumpstart Academic site is a pre-packaged website solution, providing a defined package of modules, design layouts, dynamic content types, and an easy-to-use, web-based administrative tool for content management. It is hosted on IT Services’ new Stanford Sites Drupal-hosting platform. Jumpstart Academic sites are designed to meet common functional needs of university academic departments in a visually compelling, university-branded design. A set of Stanford-branded theme options are available to choose from. Small customizations, training, user support, and site maintenance are provided.

Ideal For:

  • Larger academic or administrative departments
  • Sites that require dynamic content, such as news listings, profiles for faculty, students and staff, event calendars, and courses

Sites Personal

The quickest and easiest way for members of the Stanford community to create their own personal website for free; no coding skills necessary.

This service allows Stanford faculty, students and staff to easily create a new site on the Stanford Sites Drupal platform with little technical or design expertise required. Website owners can choose from the available themes, all of which offer design flexibility and are optimized for the needs of the community.

Stanford Sites Personal websites are ideal for:

  • faculty members
  • post-doctoral scholars
  • graduate students
